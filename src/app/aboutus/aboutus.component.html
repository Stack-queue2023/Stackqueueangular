Inclusive learning means:

  1. Provide students with a sense of belonging.
  2. Offer an open and welcoming environment.
  3. Are psychologically safe.
  4. Promote active listening.
  5. Encourage participation.
  6. Actively work to combat biases.
  7. Focus on boosting and maintaining student motivation.
